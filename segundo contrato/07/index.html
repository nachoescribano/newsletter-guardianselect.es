Guardian Glass introduces a new feature in the Performance Calculator: Embodied carbon

Guardian Glass has added a new feature to its Glass Analytics, allowing users to calculate the embodied carbon in the glass configurations they select. This tool provides architects, specifiers and glass processors with a deeper understanding of the environmental impact of the façades they design, through the glass products they specify. The CO₂ equivalent estimate applies to a wide variety of glass combinations, including float glass, coated glass, laminated glass and coated laminated glass, all produced by Guardian Glass in Europe. Visit the Guardian Glass page to learn more.

Guardian Glass and VELUX partner to develop vacuum insulating glass

Guardian Glass and the VELUX Group have established a joint development agreement for the creation of vacuum insulated glass (VIG). This collaboration will allow both companies to develop manufacturing processes and capabilities to meet the growing and changing demand for VIG.

The VIG is an innovative technology that combines energy efficiency and thermal performance. Working together, Guardian and VELUX will leverage their technical teams, intellectual property and additional tools to advance this insulating glass technology. Source: Guardian Glass.
