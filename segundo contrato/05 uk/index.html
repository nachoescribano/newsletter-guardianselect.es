New UK Building Regulations & Glass Reference Guide!

As promised in the past Webinar on Regulations, Guardian Select launches the new UK Building Regulations & Glass Reference Guide. What's new in Glass Analytics

You can now configure Guardian SatinDeco®

Guardian's SatinDeco® is a translucent, satin glass that also has a high level of natural light transmission. This not only allows plenty of light to pass through, but gently diffuses it to create environments that appear to glow.

The uniform etching of SatinDeco® also helps make marks such as fingerprints less visible.

Now you can configure it in our Guardian Glass Analytics, select the desired position and click on “Guardian Europe Residential” and change it to “Type: Textured surface” below select “Guardian SatinDeco®” and click accept.

When you issue the report, be aware of the disclaimer notice that accompanies these values.
