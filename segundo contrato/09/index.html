Guardian Glass Reduces its Carbon Footprint with New EPDs

Guardian Glass has released its latest Environmental Product Declarations (EPDs), providing a clearer understanding of the embodied CO2 (CO2e) value of its glass products and their relation to the total CO2 emissions in manufacturing.

The new flat, unprocessed glass EPD reveals a cradle-to-gate value of 1,102 kg CO2e/ton of glass, representing a 23% reduction compared to the National Glass Association’s 2019 EPD value of 1430 kg CO2e/ton and a 24% reduction compared to Guardian Glass’ 2018 EPD value of 1455 kg CO2e/ton. Additionally, new EPDs have been published for sputter-coated glass, wet coated glass, and heat-treated glass products. These advancements reflect Guardian Glass’ ongoing commitment to Environmental Stewardship. Download our EDPs.
